About

Research isn't a phase —
it's the whole argument.

I'm a multi-disciplinary designer and researcher with a background in Philosophy. My practice sits at the intersection of deep qualitative inquiry, business strategy, and participatory design — always oriented toward the question of what actually matters to the people a product serves.

I've led research across digital health, civic technology, luxury retail, education, and public policy. I believe the most impactful products are built on the clearest understanding of people — and that translating that understanding into language stakeholders can act on is its own craft.

Before design, I studied philosophy at National Taiwan University and received my MFA in Transdisciplinary Design from Parsons School of Design, New York.
