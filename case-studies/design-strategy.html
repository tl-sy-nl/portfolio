Social Innovation Strategic Foresight Facilitation

Design Thinking & Foresight Programs for Public Institutions

Over five years, I facilitated 10+ design thinking and strategic foresight programs for universities, NGOs, and public institutions — helping organizations translate complex social challenges into actionable design opportunities.

Role Design Strategist & Lead Facilitator
Duration 2020 — 2025
Sectors Education, NGO, Government
Focus Social Innovation & Sustainability

Context

As an independent design strategist, I worked with a range of public-sector and educational organizations facing complex, systemic challenges — from sustainable development to social innovation and digital transformation. These organizations often had deep domain expertise but lacked frameworks for human-centered problem-framing and participatory ideation.

My role was to design and facilitate structured programs that bridged the gap between institutional knowledge and actionable design strategy, making futures thinking and user-centered approaches accessible to non-design audiences.

Approach

Human-Centered Strategy Consulting

Each engagement began with deep contextual understanding — mapping stakeholder ecosystems, identifying latent tensions, and framing challenges in ways that made them designable. I provided research consulting that helped organizations see their problems through the lens of the people they serve, translating abstract institutional goals into concrete user needs and opportunity spaces.

Cross-Disciplinary Workshop Design

I designed and facilitated workshops that brought together researchers, policymakers, practitioners, and community members. The emphasis was on creating psychologically safe spaces for divergent thinking, then guiding groups toward convergent, actionable outcomes. Formats ranged from half-day sprints to multi-week design programs.

Program Themes & Methods

  • Strategic foresight and scenario planning for public policy
  • Design thinking sprints for social innovation challenges
  • Participatory research methods and co-creation frameworks
  • Stakeholder alignment workshops for cross-sector collaboration
  • Speculative design exercises for long-term futures exploration

Impact

Across 10+ programs, I helped organizations shift from reactive problem-solving to proactive, research-informed strategy. Key outcomes included actionable design briefs adopted by participating institutions, new cross-sector collaborations formed through workshop connections, and sustained adoption of human-centered methods within partner organizations.

This body of work deepened my conviction that the most powerful design interventions often happen before any interface is designed — in the framing of the question, the alignment of stakeholders, and the creation of shared language across disciplines.

Highlights

  • 10+ programs facilitated across universities, NGOs, and public institutions
  • Focus areas spanning social innovation, sustainable development, and digital transformation
  • Co-founded the Taiwan chapter of The Design Futures Initiative
  • Work presented at MoMA and the Biodesign Challenge Summit (2019)
